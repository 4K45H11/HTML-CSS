Biography

Virat Kohli born on November 5, 1988 in Delhi, India.He made his debut in Indian cricket team in August 2008 and quickly established himself as a prolific run scorer. Known for his aggressive bating style, and strong leadership, he has set numerous records and achieved great success as the captain of Indian cricket team.

He is one of the great legends of Indian cricket team.
