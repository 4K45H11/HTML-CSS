Biography

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi commonly known as Mahatma Gandhi, was a prominent leader in Indian independence movement.He born on October 2 1869 porbandar, India.He known for his philosophy of non-violence and civil disobedience, which he called "satyagraha".

Philosophy

Ahimsha (Non Vilolence)

Gandhi believed the power of non violence is a means to bring social and political change.He practiced and preached the principle of "Ahimsha",which indicates non violent resistant even in the face of opression.

Satyagraha (Truth Force)

The concept is to persue truth and justice through non violence means.It became a cornerstone of his struggle for India's independence.

Legacy

Mahatma Gandhi's is profund and continues to inspire people worldwide.His non vilolence approach to change has influenced civil rights movement,leaders,and activities globally.He played a pivotal role for India's fight for independence from British rule and often reffered as "Father of the nation" in India.
