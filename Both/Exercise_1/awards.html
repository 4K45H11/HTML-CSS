Notable Awards and Honors

  • Knight Commender of the Order of the British Empire(KBE). Bill Gates recieved this honor in 2002 for his contributions to the technology industry and global philanthropy.
  • Albert Einstine Technology Medal - Bill Gates recieved this for changing and improving the technology, education sectors.
  • Padma Bhushan - Govt of India awarded him Padma Bhushan for his social contributions in 2015.
  • Presidential Medal of Freedom.-In 2016 He was given this award by President Barack Obama.
  • Legion of Honour - The French Govt. awarded Bill Gates, this honour in 2017, for his contribution in science and society.
  • 2018: Professor Honoris Causa from the University of Cambridge.
