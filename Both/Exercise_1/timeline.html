Key Events in the Life of Bill Gates

  • 1975: Co-founded microsoft with Paul Allen.
  • 1980: Signed a deal with IBM for MS-DOS.
  • 1985: Launched Windows 1.0.
  • 1994: Married Melinda French.
  • 1995: Windows 95 launched with start menu, creating new era for personal computing
  • 2000: Established Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.
  • 2008: Tranitioned from full-time work at microsoft to focus on philanthropy.
  • 2010: With Warren Buffet pladged to use most of their wealth for philanthropy.
  • 2014: Steps down as microsoft chairman, stays as technology advisor.
  • 2021: Bill and Melinda Gates announced their divorce.
