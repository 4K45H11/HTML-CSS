Early Life

Bill Gates is an American business magnet,software developer,philanthropist.Born on October 28, 1955 in seattle, Washington. He showed interest in computers in very early age, created his first computer program at the age of 13.

Co-founding Microsoft

In 1975 along with his childhood friend Paul Allen Bill founded microsoft which revolusionized the computer world.Microsoft's operating system and MS-DOS beacme the foundation of their success.

Software Innovations

Under the leadership of Bill Gates they launched Windows , the graphical version of MS-DOS.It beacame the most used operating system for personal computers, and MS Office a productivity software, further solidifies the company's position in the industry.

Philanthropy

Bill Gates along with his formal life Melinda, established the Bill & Melinda Gates foundation in 2000. The foundation focuses on the global issues like healthcare, poverty and education. It has donated billions to verious causes.

Global Impact

Bill gates has had a profound impact on technology, bussiness and philanthropy.His visions leaderships, and contribution continues to shape the mordern world, making him one of the most influential figures in the tech industry.
