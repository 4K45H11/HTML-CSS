Attractions of Mumbai City

Gateway of India

A historic monument built in British Raj overlooking Arabian sea.

Locations: Apollo Bunder Colaba.

Chhatrapati Sivaji Terminus

A UNESCO World heritage site and a prominent railway station.

Locations: Fort, Mumbai.

Marine Drive

A picturesque promenade along the Arabian Sea, known as Queen's necklace.

Locations: Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose road, South Mumbai.
