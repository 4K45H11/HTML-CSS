History of Mumbai City

Mumbai formaly known as Bombay, has a rich history that dates back centuries.Here are key milestones that shaped the city :

Ancient Period

Mumbai has evidence of human habitance dating back to stone age.The city was full of islands and inhabited by koli fishing communities.

Colonial Era

Portuguese followed by British had a significant impact on Mumbai.British East India Company developed the city as a trading hub,and Mumbai quickly grew in importance.

Independence and Post-Independence

Mumbai played a crusial role in India's stuggle for independence. After gaining freedom in 1947, Mumbai became capital of newly formed state of Maharastra in 1960.

Economic Hub

over the years Mumbai became financial , commercial and entertainment hub of India.The city's skyline dotted with sky scrapers and it continues to be a melting pot of diverse culture and communities.
