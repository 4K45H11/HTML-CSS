Explore the vibrancy of Mumbai City

Mumbai, formaly known as Bombay is the financial, commercial and entertainment capital of India.This city is known for it's historic landmarks,bustling market,diverse cuisine and lively events.

Immerse yourself in the rich history of Mumbai, visit iconic attractions, indulge in local cuisine and explore the city's vibrant events.Discover the interest facts and explore the convenient transportation that make mumbai truely unique.

Interesting facts about Mumbai

  • Mumbai is the home of Bollywood Industry one of the biggest film industry in Asia.
  • The city is build on reclaimed islands and has a robust costal area.
  • Chhatrapati Sivaji Terminus(CST) is a UNESCO World heritage site and a prominent railway station.
  • Mumbai's local train network is one of the busiest in the world, connecting millions of commuters daily.
  • Gateway of India an iconic monument, was built to commemorate the visit of King Gorge V and Queen Mary to Mumbai.

Transportation in Mumbai

Mumbai offers variety of trasportaion options, including:

  • Local Trains: The lifeline of mumbai connection the cities suburban areas.
  • BEST Buses: The Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport(BEST) bus service covers the entire city.
  • Metro: Metro provides quick transport in certain areas.
  • Taxis and Auto Ricksaws: Widely available for convenient point to point travel.
  • Ferries: Connecting the city to costal areas and nearby islands.
