Mumbai City Cuisine

Mumbai is a culinary paradise with a diverse range of flavours.Here are some must try dishes and food experiences in the city :

Pav Bhaji

A popular street food dish consisting of spicy vegetables curry(bhaji) served with soft bread rolls(pav).

Bhel Puri

A delightful combination of puffed rice,sev,chutneys and verious ingredients creating a testy snack.

Vada Pav

Known as Indian Burger it consists of spicy potato filler(vada) placed inside a pav.
